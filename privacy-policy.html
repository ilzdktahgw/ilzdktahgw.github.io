Privacy Policy

Last updated on December 18, 2023

This Privacy Policy applies to this website and all apps made by Justln Apps Inc. and describes the way Justln Apps Inc. collects and uses your data.

What data is collected and how is it used?
Justln Apps Inc. does not collect any data from you. However, third party service providers used in the apps may automatically collect some non-personal data For example, they may collect device information such as your device model and the operating system, IP address to estimate the device's general location, product interactions such as app launches and taps, and diagnostic information such as crash logs and energy usage. Data collected from third party service providers may be used for analytics, fraud prevention, and to serve advertisements. Data security, retention, and deletion policy
Justln Apps Inc. does not collect or store any of your data. Data collected by third party service providers is securly stored by them. Please refer to the links above for more information on how third party service providers store, retain, and delete your data.

California residents, data protection rights under GDPR, and children
No personal data is collected from any user. Personalized advertising is disabled for all users by tagging all ad requests as child directed. This means that ads are served without using any personal data The max ad content rating for all users is set to G (general).

Justln Apps Inc. does not collect or store any data of your data from this website. However, GitHub Pages is used to host this website. Your IP address may be collected and stored by them for security purposes. For more information on how GitHub handles your data, please see their Privacy Policy.

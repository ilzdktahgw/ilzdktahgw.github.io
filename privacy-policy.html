Privacy Policy

Last updated on December 18, 2023

This Privacy Policy applies to all apps made by Justln Apps Inc. and describes the way Justln Apps Inc. collects and uses your personal data.

What data is collected and how is it used?
Justln Apps Inc. does not collect any data from you. However, third party service providers used in the apps may automatically collect some non-personal information. For example, they may collect device information such as your device model and the operating system, IP address to estimate the device's general location, product interactions such as app launches and taps, diagnostic information such as crash logs and energy usage, and device identifiers. Data collected from third party service providers may be used for analytics, fraud prevention, and to serve advertisements. Data security
Justln Apps Inc. does not store any of your data. Data collected by third party service providers is securly stored by them.

California residents, data protection rights under GDPR, and children
Personalized advertising is disabled for all users by tagging all ad requests as child directed. This means that ads are served without using any personal information. Also, the max ad content rating for all users is set to G (general).
